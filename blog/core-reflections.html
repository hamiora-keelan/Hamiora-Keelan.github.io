Core Reflections

What is core learning

At Dev Academy, core learning extends beyond the technical skills typically associated with software development. The program encompasses a range of essential skills, including mindfulness, mindset cultivation, learning strategies, and problem-solving techniques.

Self discovery

Exploring core concepts has been instrumental in my journey of self-discovery. I've come to realize that my problem-solving approach isn't as rigidly methodical as I initially believed. Through this process, I've uncovered my proficiency in coding and have become increasingly at ease with refining my foundational skills to enhance my capabilities. This realization has empowered me to embrace and develop my strengths further, fostering a deeper sense of confidence in my abilities.

Values , empathy and self-awareness in learning and programming

Mastering programming involves encountering numerous challenges, which may at times seem like towering obstacles. Thus, it's crucial to cultivate mindfulness. This mindfulness fosters presence, enabling one to recognize when it's prudent to pause and reassess. Empathy facilitates collaboration with others, while values provide a sturdy foundation for identity and purpose.

Core learning surprises

Approaching Dev Academy I expected to be learning soft skills relevant to the industry but I was surprised by the depth that Dev Academy has gone into. I really enjoyed having to write blogs, it has helped to consolidate the knowledge we have learnt.

Core learning challenges

It has been difficult to learn topics that I have learnt before as I have trouble separating the previous teaching and learning the topic fresh.

Core learnings, the key to dev academy success

I think the reason Dev Academy has placed an emphasis on core learnings is their solution to getting students into dev positions and this is reflected in their high success rate. Dev academy believes that tech employers are looking for exceptional human beings, not just another expert coder.

Is core learning time well spent

Core learnings have been integral to my development, and I believe I have much to offer as a human being. I don't aspire to be a genius programmer, but I intend to leverage my exceptional communication skills to add value to any future employers
