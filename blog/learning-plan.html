How I will manage my workload

I am dedicated to continuous learning and ensuring that the educational needs of my peers are met. To adhere to time constraints, I utilize my phone timer, striving to align with suggested timeboxes.

While I occasionally extend beyond the allocated time during more technical tasks, I always do so within reasonable limits, avoiding unsustainable overtime.

I will use empathy to provide honest feedback. I will remain open to feedback given by my peers and facilitators.

If I feel overwhelmed I will make sure to seek help from my facilitators.
