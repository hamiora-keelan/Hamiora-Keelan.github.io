Positioning explained: Static, Relative, Absolute and Fixed

Introduction

Without positioning, you might as well be living your life in 2d. Mastering the positioning properties will give you the freedom to move content to your will. Most programs have a default setting, this also applies to HTML and CSS. The default property for CSS positioning is Static. The Static property positions elements in the same order that they were written in HTML.
