Blog questions

Explain a situation where you have made an ethical decision. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and how you reflect on the decision now?

During my climb to restaurant manager, had made a good friends with a front of house manager, they grew to be a great mentor for me. During my first year as the manager for the restaurant I came across a difficult situation. Unfortunately this friend had broken a zero tolerance food safety policy on multiple occasions and I had no choice but to terminate them. I had taken into consideration their relationship with me, along with the strength of their skills in coaching and despite their value to the business and to me, I had to follow through with the termination.

Share an example of when you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss strategies you tried at the time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now and why?

During a communication group project, we divided roles among six team members. Unfortunately, one of our teammates struggled to meet the set deadlines, affecting the overall progress. Reflecting on this experience, I believe that for my next team project, establishing clear expectations from the beginning and actively listening to the limitations of each team member would enhance our understanding and enable us to support one another more effectively.
